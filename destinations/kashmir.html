Full Itinerary Details

Day 1: Arrival Srinagar

Pickup from Srinagar Airport. Transfer to Houseboat. Evening enjoy a 1-hour Shikara Ride on Dal Lake, visiting the floating market and Nehru Park. Dinner and overnight stay in the Houseboat.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahi. Visit Shankaracharya Temple located on a hillock offering panoramic views of the city.

Day 3: Gulmarg Day Trip

Drive to Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers). Take the famous Gondola Cable Car Ride (Phase 1 & 2) up to Apharwat Peak. Enjoy snow activities. Return to Srinagar hotel by evening.

Day 4: Pahalgam Excursion

Drive to Pahalgam (Valley of Shepherds). En route, visit Saffron fields and Avantipura ruins. In Pahalgam, visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari. Return to Srinagar.

Day 5: Sonamarg Day Trip

Drive to Sonamarg (Meadow of Gold). Visit Thajiwas Glacier where snow remains round the year. You can hire ponies to go up to the glacier. Return to Srinagar.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your return flight with sweet memories of Paradise on Earth.
