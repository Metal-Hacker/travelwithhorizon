Goa Beach & Party

₹30,000 / Person

Full Itinerary Details

Day 1: Arrival in Goa

Pickup from Dabolim Airport/Mopa Airport. Transfer to your hotel in North Goa. Welcome drink on arrival. Evening free to stroll at Calangute/Baga Beach and enjoy the beach shacks.

Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing

Full day tour of North Goa beaches: Candolim, Baga, and Anjuna. Visit Fort Aguada and the Lighthouse. Water sports activities like parasailing and jet skiing available at Calangute (direct payment).

Day 3: South Goa Heritage

Drive to South Goa. Visit Old Goa Churches (Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral). Visit Mangueshi Temple. In the evening, enjoy a sunset river cruise on the Mandovi River with folk dance performances.

Day 4: Dudhsagar Waterfalls or Leisure

Optional jeep safari to Dudhsagar Waterfalls (seasonal) OR a day at leisure to rent a scooter and explore local cafes, Thalassa, and Chapora Fort (Dil Chahta Hai Fort).

Day 5: Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Check-out and transfer to the airport. Buy some cashew nuts and Feni as souvenirs before you fly back.
