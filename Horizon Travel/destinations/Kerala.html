Tour Itinerary (5 Days / 4 Nights)

Day 1: Arrival in Cochin

Arrival at Cochin Airport. Transfer to Munnar (4 hours drive). Check-in at hotel and leisure time.

Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing

Visit the famous Tea Gardens, Mattupetty Dam, and Eravikulam National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr.

Day 3: Thekkady Wildlife

Transfer to Thekkady. Boat ride in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Spice plantation tour in the evening.

Day 4: Alleppey Houseboat

Drive to Alleppey. Check into a traditional Houseboat. Cruise through the backwaters and enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine.

Day 5: Departure

Morning breakfast on the houseboat. Transfer back to Cochin Airport for your onward journey.
