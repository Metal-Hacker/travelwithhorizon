Royal Rajasthan

₹38,000 / Person

Full Itinerary Details

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur (Pink City)

Pick up from Jaipur Airport/Railway Station. Transfer to hotel. In the evening, we visit the Birla Mandir (White Marble Temple) and Chokhi Dhani for an authentic Rajasthani dinner with cultural folk dance.

Day 2: Jaipur Historical Tour

Full day sightseeing. Morning excursion to Amber Fort (Elephant ride optional). On the way back, stop at Jal Mahal. Post lunch, visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar (Observatory), and Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Evening free for shopping in Johari Bazaar.

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur (Blue City)

Morning drive to Jodhpur (approx 6 hours). Upon arrival, check in to your hotel. Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace museum in the evening. Overnight stay at Jodhpur.

Day 4: Jodhpur Sightseeing & Transfer to Udaipur

Visit the mighty Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Later, we drive towards Udaipur. En route, we visit the stunning Ranakpur Jain Temples, famous for their 1444 marble pillars. Arrival in Udaipur by evening.

Day 5: Udaipur (City of Lakes)

Morning city tour covering City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon Ki Bari. In the evening, enjoy a romantic boat ride on Lake Pichola during sunset, viewing the Jag Mandir Island Palace.

Day 6: Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to Udaipur Airport for your onward flight home. Tour concludes with royal memories.
