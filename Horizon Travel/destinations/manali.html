Manali & Kasol Vibe

₹25,000 / Person

Full Itinerary Details

Day 1: Arrival in Manali

Arrive in Manali (via Volvo bus or private cab). Check-in to your riverside hotel. Afternoon visit to Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, and the Tibetan Monastery. Evening stroll at Mall Road.

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventure

Full day excursion to Solang Valley. Indulge in adventure activities like Paragliding, Zorbing, and ATV rides (cost extra). Enjoy the breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks.

Day 3: Atal Tunnel & Sissu

Drive through the engineering marvel Atal Tunnel to reach Lahaul Valley. Visit Sissu Waterfall. The landscape changes dramatically to a rugged, cold desert terrain. Return to Manali by evening.

Day 4: Transfer to Kasol

Drive to Kasol (Parvati Valley). Visit Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and the hot springs. Check into a camp/hotel in Kasol. Evening nature walk along the Parvati River.

Day 5: Chalal Trek & Departure

Morning short trek to Chalal Village through the pine forest. Enjoy breakfast at a local cafe. In the evening, transfer to the Volvo bus stand for your overnight journey back to Delhi.
